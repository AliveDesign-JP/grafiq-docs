# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 25, 2026**

## Introduction

Grafiq ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application Grafiq (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Graph Data**: Graph names, series configurations, data points (values, dates, notes), and chart settings
- **Custom Data**: Any numerical or categorical data you manually enter into the App

### HealthKit Data

With your explicit permission, the App can read the following health data from Apple HealthKit:

- Body weight, height, BMI
- Heart rate, blood pressure, blood oxygen
- Step count, active energy

**Important**: Grafiq only reads HealthKit data. We never write to or modify your health records. HealthKit data is used solely to populate your graphs within the App and is never transmitted to any external server.

### Information Collected Automatically

We do not collect any analytics, usage data, or device information automatically. The App does not contain any tracking or analytics frameworks.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

- Provide and maintain the App's graphing and data visualization functionality
- Store your graph data locally on your device
- Sync your data across your devices via iCloud (if enabled)

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not have access to your personal data.

### iCloud Sync

If you are a Pro user and have iCloud enabled, your graph data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit Private Database. This data is:

- Protected by Apple's security measures
- Accessible only by you through your Apple ID
- Never stored in any public or shared database
- Never accessible by us or any third party

### We Do Not

- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers or analytics providers
- Collect unnecessary personal information
- Display advertisements of any kind
- Use any third-party analytics or tracking services

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services

The App uses the following Apple services:

- **StoreKit**: For processing the one-time Pro purchase
- **CloudKit**: For iCloud data sync (Pro users only, Private Database only)
- **HealthKit**: For reading health data (with your explicit permission, iPhone only)
- **WidgetKit**: For home screen widgets (Pro users only, iPhone only)

No third-party services (such as advertising networks, analytics platforms, or external APIs) are used.

## On-Device Processing

The App may use on-device machine learning (Apple Foundation Models) in the future for features such as data analysis suggestions. All such processing occurs entirely on your device. No data is sent to external servers for AI/ML processing.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data at any time within the App
- Export your data from the App
- Delete your data at any time by deleting individual graphs or uninstalling the App
- Revoke HealthKit permissions at any time via iOS Settings
- Disable iCloud sync at any time via iOS Settings
- Request information about what data we collect

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it. If iCloud sync is enabled, data persists in your iCloud account until you delete it from the App or remove it via iCloud settings.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
